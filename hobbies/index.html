When All The Pieces Fit

There is no greater pleasure than putting together a 1000 piece puzzle! The time I spend putting together a magical landscape, mythical creature or colorful scenery can be ✨therapeutic✨.

You build a magical scenery piece by piece and get to see your hard work come together as a beautiful unicorn... or enchanted cottage... or a solid color! The sweet feeling of setting down that last piece perfectly is rather magical.

Warm Cookies and Ice Cold Milk

Aside from building puzzles, I'd say my second love is eating fresh baked cookies! The warm gooey chocolate calls my name. Is there any better pairing than
fresh baked cookies and ice cold milk?

Scones

While exploring the Bay Area, I have embarked on a new journey... a Baked Goods Journey. From finding the best scones to learning how to bake them, I love everything about this crumbly, buttery, flaky masterpiece!
